SAP Finance Operations Manager - Accounts / Manager - Accounts (SAP)

Job Title: SAP Finance Operations Manager - Accounts / Manager Accounts - SAP

Industry: Manufacturing

Work Location: BKC, Mumbai, Maharashtra

Reporting to: Chief Financial Officer

Education Qualifications Required: B.Com or M.Com

Salary Budget: 10-12 LPA

Salary Offered: 20-25% hike on current salary

Work Experience Required: 5-8 Years


We're seeking a dynamic SAP Finance Operations Manager - Accounts / Manager Accounts - SAP to join our team in BKC, Bandra East, Mumbai, Maharashtra. Ideal candidates will have 5-8 years of experience in accounting and finance roles, with a strong emphasis on SAP knowledge and proficiency. The successful candidate will be responsible for automated payment processing, cash flow management, and coordination with internal teams and banking partners. Candidates from a manufacturing industry background are preferred, and residency in nearby locations is desirable. If you're motivated, detail-oriented, and ready to take on this exciting opportunity, we want to hear from you!


Requirements:

  • Minimum of 5 years of experience in accounting and finance roles.
  • SAP knowledge and experience of at least 5 years.
  • Proficiency in MS Excel/MS Office with a minimum of 5 years of experience.
  • Experience with automated payment processing (F110) through SAP.
  • Experience in cash flow management.
  • Familiarity with online/CMS platforms of banking.
  • Candidates from a manufacturing industry background are preferred.
  • Candidates must be residing in nearby locations.

Responsibilities:

  • Proficient in automated payment processing through SAP.
  • Ability to process advance payments through SAP.
  • Skilled in preparing daily and monthly cash flow reports with variance reporting.
  • Competent in daily upload of bank statements and bank reconciliations.
  • Capable of posting all payments and collections in SAP, including import payments and export collections, and posting journal vouchers.
  • Able to verify and post bank charges and interest in SAP.
  • Experience in vendor registrations, verification of processed payments, and authorizations on CMS platforms of banks.
  • Strong coordination and liaison skills with internal teams and bankers for smooth operations.
  • Ability to manage working capital requirements and surplus in day-to-day operations.
  • Adherence to SOPs, guidelines, and best practices for functional responsibilities.
  • Proficiency in generating MIS reports and other reporting duties as required.

Notice Period: 30 Days

Work Mode: Work From Office

Interview Process: 1VC and Final F2F
