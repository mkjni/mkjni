SAP Business One Technical Developer

Job Title: SAP Business One Technical Developer

Industry: IT Consulting

Work Location: Sholinganallur, Chennai, Tamil Nadu.

Experience Required: 5 - 8 Years

Salary Offered: 13 - 16 LPA


Job Summary:

  • We are seeking a skilled SAP Business One Technical Developer to join our team. In this role, you will be responsible for developing, customizing, and maintaining SAP Business One solutions using SAP B1 SDK.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and customize SAP Business One solutions using SAP B1 SDK (Software Development Kit) and DI-API (Data Interface API).
  • Integrate SAP B1 with other third-party applications using APIs, web services, and middleware.
  • Design and develop custom modules, add-ons, and extensions using .NET programming languages such as C# or VB.NET.
  • Create and maintain SQL queries, stored procedures, and database objects to support SAP B1 data management and reporting requirements.
  • Collaborate with functional consultants and business analysts to gather requirements, design technical solutions, and implement enhancements.
  • Develop and maintain reports, dashboards, and analytics using SAP Crystal Reports, SQL Server Reporting Services (SSRS), or other reporting tools.
  • Perform data migration, data integration, and data synchronization tasks between SAP B1 and other systems.
  • Provide technical support, troubleshooting, and resolution of issues related to SAP B1 solutions and integrations.
  • Conduct code reviews, performance tuning, and optimization to ensure the scalability, reliability, and performance of SAP B1 solutions.
  • Deep knowledge of SAP B1 cloud and on-premises installations.

Requirements:

  • Total experience of 5 to 8 years with a minimum of 5 years of experience in SAP Business One development and customization.
  • Handling of at least 5 end-to-end SAP B1 implementations.
  • Proficiency in SAP B1 SDK, DI-API, and development tools.
  • Strong programming skills in .NET languages such as C# or VB.NET.
  • Experience with SQL Server or other relational database management systems.
  • Knowledge of SAP B1 modules such as Finance, Sales, Purchasing, Inventory, and Production.
  • SAP B1 certification(s) are a plus.

Preferences:

  • Immediate joiners preferred.
  • Position involves immediate onsite travel and frequent travel to customer sites.
  • Possession of a passport is mandatory.
  • Office Location: Sholinganallur Chennai, and NO work from home.

Please apply only if you meet all the above requirements.
