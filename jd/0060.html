Lead Execution Specialist / Event Operations Manager

Role: Lead Execution Specialist

Job ID: 0060

Industry: Wedding

Job Function: Operations

Location: Indiranagar, Bengaluru

Education Qualification: Graduate

Experience Required: 5 - 8 Years

Salary Offered: 14 - 18 LPA


Position Overview: We are seeking a dynamic and experienced Lead Execution Specialist to oversee the seamless operation of our online platform. The ideal candidate will be responsible for managing all aspects of the operational workflow, including client interactions, project timelines, and team coordination. This role requires exceptional communication skills, meticulous attention to detail, and a proven track record of successfully executing tasks in a fast-paced environment.


Responsibilities:

  • Interact with clients to understand their needs and preferences, ensuring a personalized and satisfactory experience throughout the planning process.
  • Monitor project timelines and deadlines to ensure all tasks are completed on schedule.
  • Coordinate with program managers, wedding planners, and other team members to facilitate smooth workflow and timely updates.
  • Serve as the main point of contact for all operational inquiries and concerns, providing prompt and effective solutions as needed.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify areas for process improvement and implement efficient solutions.
  • Conduct regular performance evaluations to assess team productivity and identify areas for skill development or additional support.
  • Stay updated on industry trends, competitor activities, and market demands to maintain a competitive edge in the market.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Marketing, Hospitality Management, or a related field.
  • Proven experience in a similar operational role, preferably within the wedding, events, paint, or furniture industry.
  • Exceptional communication skills, both written and verbal, with the ability to effectively interact with clients and team members.
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail, with the ability to multitask and prioritize tasks in a fast-paced environment.
  • Demonstrated leadership abilities, with the capacity to motivate and inspire team members to achieve goals and objectives.
  • Flexible and adaptable to changing priorities and business needs.
  • Passion for the wedding and events industry, with a commitment to delivering exceptional service and experiences to clients.

Notice Period: 15 Days


Required Skills: Excellent Communication. Event Coordination. Monitor event project timelines and deadlines. Client Coordination. Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
