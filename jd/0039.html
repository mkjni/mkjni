Customer Support Executive

Position: Customer Support Executive

Job ID: 0039

Industry: Accounting, Billing, and Inventory Management Software.

Work Location: HSR Layout, Bengaluru, Karnataka.

Experience Required: 0-4 Years

Education Qualification: Graduate

Compensation Offered: Salary up to 22,000 Per Month and Health Insurance benefits for the candidate and his/her parents.


Role Summary:

We are looking for passionate and motivated individuals to join our Customer Support Team. Your main responsibility is to handle customer questions, comments, and complaints regarding a particular business. The ultimate goal is to provide positive customer experiences by enhancing relationships between them.


Key Responsibilities:

  • Should be able to answer incoming customer calls, take appropriate action for each call, and ensure there are no escalations in the service provided.
  • Should be able to answer emails timely, ensuring adherence to process standards.
  • Follow up on customer calls made wherever necessary.
  • Maintain customer satisfaction ratings based on explicit criteria set forth by the company.
  • Attend mandatory training sessions to stay updated on product or company policy changes.
  • Use company policies to determine if there can be an immediate resolution to a customer issue or if that issue requires managerial input.
  • Input data into the company system.

Must-have:

  • Verbal and written communication skills in Hindi and English languages.
  • Problem analysis and problem-solving abilities.
  • Good data entry and typing skills.
  • Listening skills.
  • Attention to detail.
  • Teamwork.
  • High level of professionalism.

Work Mode: Work From Office

Working Schedule: 6 Days working - Day Shift.

Notice Period: Immediate Joiners Preferred.

Interview Process: Face-to-Face Interview at HSR Layout.
