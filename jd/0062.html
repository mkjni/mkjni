Corporate Sales Manager (B2B, SaaS)

Job Title: Corporate Sales Manager (B2B, SaaS)

Experience Required: 2 - 7 years

Work Location: Telangana, Hyderabad and Delhi


About Job Nexus India:

We are a trusted recruitment partner for companies and consultancies across India. We leverage our extensive network and expertise to source, screen, and submit top talent that aligns perfectly with their requirements.


About the company we are hiring for:

The company offers car parking, traffic challan payments, car wash, Fastag, Car Trade, Insurance services. They are hiring for this role to target corporates as their customers. The candidate will visit corporates and sell these services.


Role & Responsibilities:

  • Establish Sales Network:
    • Identify and develop new markets (Tier 2/3 cities/Zones) in the region to drive the business targets of the region.
    • Manage and drive relationships with corporate clientele (existing and potential) in your city/region to enable growth and revenue objectives.
    • Build a healthy portfolio of partners/affiliates/agencies to drive corporate acquisition - creating revenue-generating partners.
  • Operational Efficiencies:
    • Build, track, and manage a pipeline of sales opportunities.
    • Achievement of sales within Commercial Complex, Corporate & Partners.
    • Monitor performance through input KPIs on a daily basis.
    • Coordinate site installations between customers and Park+ cross-functional teams.
  • Customer Focus:
    • Understand customer requirements and articulate problem statements to pitch/build the right product.
    • Develop a deep and long-standing relationship with KDMs within the customer portfolio to maximize the life-cycle value.
    • Build a pipeline for upsell and cross-sell to existing accounts.

Ideal Candidate:

  • Preferably 1 to 3 years of experience in Corporate sales.
  • A flair for problem-solving with strong organizational skills.
  • Proven track record of owning responsibility and scaling up teams.
  • Ability to measure and analyze key performance indicators (ROI and KPIs).
  • Ability to work as an individual contributor and drive the sales strategy for the team.
  • Startup experience is a plus.

Note: This role requires client visits.

Note: For Hyderabad Location only Hyderabad Candidates.

Note: For Delhi Location only Delhi Candidates.


Working Days: 6 days/week

Maximum Notice Period: 30 Days
