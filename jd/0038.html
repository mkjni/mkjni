Executive or Senior Executive - Treasury

Role: Executive or Senior Executive - Treasury

Job ID: 0038

Industry: Manufacturing, B2B and Retail.

Work Location: Bandra East, Mumbai, Maharashtra

Experience Required: 3 - 5 Years

Education Qualification: Bcom + MBA in Finance

Salary Offered: Best in the industry.


Requirements:

  • Must have 3 to 5 years of experience in a treasury role.
  • Work experience in relevant industry.
  • Experience in cash flow and income projections
  • Experience in internal audit of investments
  • Experience in accounting of all transactions of investments
  • Experience in IRR reporting and portfolio review

Responsibilities:

  • Cash flow projections for all individuals/Entities.
  • Income projections, monitoring, and quarterly review of the same for all individuals/Entities
  • Internal audit of investments purchase/sale prices and costs/expenses associated with it for all individuals/Entities.
  • Monthly verification of all investments with mutual fund statements/Demat statements and balance confirmations
  • Accounting for all transactions of investments/others for all individuals/Entities
  • Internal Rate of Return (IRR) reporting and portfolio review
  • Transactions execution for all individuals/Entities
  • Proper agreements/Documentation to be filed/maintained for all individuals/Entities.

Note: Candidates residing in Mumbai, and nearby locations are preferred.


Mandatory Skills Required: Cash Flow Projection, Internal Rate of Return (IRR), Treasury
